About Us




Our Goal

As a top Missoula dispensary, Camille's Cannabis strives to provide the best quality medical and recreational cannabis, extracts, and infused products available at afforable prices with compassion that can only come from those who are knowledgable in the field.

This goal guides us in every step we take in our business, from the types of products we offer to the way we approach our relationships with customers.




History

Camille's Cannabis opened in 2022 in Missoula, Montana. It is one of the few woman owned dispensaries in the area and we proudly dedicate our work to the medical and recreational marijuana community. We are currently in the process of opening other locations across the state.




Services

Camille's Cannabis grows all of their flower in-house and offers a variety of unique strains to meet all your need medical (and non-medical) needs. We offer different types of marijuana products such as edibles, concentrates, and flower. All of Camille's Cannabis products are vigorously tested by a state certified laboratory.
