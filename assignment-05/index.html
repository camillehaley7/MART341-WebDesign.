Impractical Jokers

Impractical Jokers is a TV show about four friends who take dares to the next level.They compete with each other to see who can the most challenges in order to avoid being the Biggest Loser.

At the end of the challenges, whoever lost the most, becomes the biggest loser and has to complete a very embarrassing dare that they cannot say no to.
